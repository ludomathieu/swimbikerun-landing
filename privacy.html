SWIM BIKE RUN

Privacy
Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The short version: Swim Bike Run does not collect, store or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Collection

Swim Bike Run does not collect any personal data. We do not use analytics, tracking, or any third-party data collection services within the app.

All information you enter in the app — race names, dates, checklist items, debrief notes — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your iPhone using AsyncStorage. This data is never transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

If you delete the app, all associated data is permanently deleted from your device.

Notifications

Swim Bike Run may request permission to send you local push notifications for race reminders. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external server.

Calendar Access

Swim Bike Run may request access to your calendar to create race reminder events. This access is used solely to add events to your local calendar. No calendar data is read, stored or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Swim Bike Run does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We will never introduce data collection without clearly informing users.
